Games

Games have been a big part of my life, as started with a gameboy it became a very big hobby as it connected me with friends and people all over the world.

Below I will show some of the franchises that I really enjoyed throughout the years.

Pokemon

A franchise that everyone has heard about. It was a big hit and everyone enjoyed it. As a child, pokemon was the first game I ever played and with how much I enjoyed it I have bought every generation of the pokemon game to date. It might get old from time to time but the they keep improving and adding new features and of course the most important New Pokemons.

Call of Duty

Every kid has played CoD or Halo, depending on your console. But back in the day after school I went home turned on the playstation 3 and joined friends in voice chat and played some games of call of duty, either online, zombies or survival mode. It was such a simple time where winning or losing didn't matter and it was all about having fun. And every year they released a new one with new guns, features and modes.

Fire Emblem

Fire Emblem is a strategic war simulator, moving troops on the battle field fighting other troops. its an easy rock paper scissor game where you try to get your opponent's HP to 0 and seize the map. But it stops being easy when stats and chance comes into play; HP, Attack Magic Speed Skill Luck Defence Resistance, Critical hit, Dodges ,terrain, etc. All these stats that affect gameplay in one way or another. With Permadeath meaning after a unit dies it is gone for good, making you think about every move you make.

Super Smash Bros.

Have you ever wanted to beat one nintendo character with another? Well this is it. A unique touchh on the fighting game genre. Instead of actual HP bar that gets lowered its everytime you hit someone their % increases, with the higher percentage meaning the higher the knock back untill they get thrown off the map or smashed out of the 4 blast zones. With recent additions of non-Nintendo characters people from so many different games can join the roster and enjoy the Smash.

Tales of

A typical JRPG (Japanese Role Playing Game) with a main character, new party members that join throughout the journey, a story to go through and countless battles. with over 15 main installments the basic gameplay remains but every game has it's unique spin on it.
