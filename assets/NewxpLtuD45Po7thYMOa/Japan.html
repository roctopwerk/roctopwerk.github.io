Japan

Anime

Anime has been a big part of my life and it's not just pokemon but tons of other series that I really enjoy. Wanting to go to Japan because of it but once I got there. Anime was only a small part of why I enjoy Japan so much.

Hiking

Last year I was hiking in California with some friends and it was a lot of fun going through the forest, up hills and finding a great view, taking pictures to remember and some amazing landscapes that can only be seen on top of them. This year I didn't had the chance to climb mount Fuji but next time I will climb it and see a great view from atop.

Cherry Blossom Trees

More known as Sakura Trees, these trees only bloom their beautiful pink blossoms in the mid of spring, for a couple of weeks they are everywhere throughout the land but sadly they only last for a couple of weeks. Making planning ahead a thing if you want to see them in Japan. Next time I will visit Japan is gonna be right before these Trees bloom.

Culture

The Japanese culture is a very unique one. As they are quite isolated from the world and live their own life on this Island they have their own culture. They slowly take stuff from outside into their country but you can see that everyone who lives there has respect and follows the culture. It's an unique and cool experience to see it, people actually lining up for trains and busses. Trains being on time by the second and everyone showing respect to one another. It was really as if you were in another world.
