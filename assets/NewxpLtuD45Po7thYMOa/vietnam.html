Vietnam

City life

In various cities you can find street markets. Where they sell fresh made food for a very cheap price. cities are usually close a beach for foreigners as vietnam gets a lot from tourist.

Farmers

Vietnam is a country that has a ton of farmers. Food like rice, wheat and meat usually come from these places. every day they eat rice in a way. cooked rice, phở (which is rice noodles), Xoi (sweet rice with sugar, peanuts, cocos and food coloring)

Night Life

Vietnam is very active in the night. That's because during the day it can get up to 40 Degrees Celcius and people take shelter from the sun around 12-3PM. Making night the ideal time to go outside. people sell tons of food, go on long walks on the beach and drink with some friends.

The Sea

Vietnam is known for making the most freshest of food. That is because everyday people will buy fresh ingredients at the market. But what you see at markets are a lot of fish, from crabs, squids etc. Because they are quite the delicacy.
